What's inside

One-handed counting

Tap the card to count. Swipe to switch dhikr. Undo keeps your count safe.

99 Names + 60 duas

Asma ul Husna, Salawat, Ayatul Kursi, morning & evening adhkar — all built in.

Add your own dhikr

Title, meaning, target, pause point. Full Arabic (RTL) text field. Unlimited.

Targets & pauses

Set 33, 99, 100 or your own. Soft haptic at each pause. Green when goal is reached.

Built for everyone

5 text size levels, generous touch targets, optional Eastern Arabic numerals.

Three languages

Turkish, English and Arabic — with full right-to-left layout.
